Our Story

Connecting Campus Trade

EduShop was founded as an Integrated Design Project (IDP) with the goal of creating a secure, trusted, and efficient peer-to-peer marketplace specifically designed for students, research lab coordinators, and faculty members.

P2P Exchange

We facilitate the exchange of academic materials, textbooks, lab kits, electronic components, and sportswear within the campus boundaries, removing middleman fees.

Quality Audits

Our platform features a central Logistics Service Team who verify the quality and specs of high-value lab equipment before completing the transaction, reducing discrepancy risks.

Ecosystem Role Profiles

🎓

Customer

Students or researchers who browse catalogs, buy lab kits, lock products in carts, submit orders, and submit rating reviews.

🏪

Seller

Verified students, departments, or lab coordinators listing excess inventories, uploading product images, and handling transactions.

📦

Service Team

Campus inventory agents auditing quality specs, identifying discrepancies, managing deliveries, and updating the transaction log.

🛡️

Administrator

System coordinators managing categories, auditing platform activity, reviewing conflict logs, and monitoring general platform analytics.

Our Mission

To create a completely frictionless, secure, and circular e-commerce ecosystem that promotes sustainability, sharing resources, and educational accessibility across the entire campus community.
