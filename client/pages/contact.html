Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Logistics Quality Control (QC) review work? +
Once a Student Customer places an order, the system reserves the vendor's stock. The physical product must then be brought to the campus Logistics Service Desk. Our agents verify that the model, hardware state, and accessories match the description listed by the seller.
Who can register as a Seller? +
Any registered college student, research laboratory assistant, department administration officer, or student sports leader is authorized to register and upgrade their role to a Vendor/Seller. Account approvals are managed by system administrators.
How are payments processed? +
Currently, the platform locks the trade logs for audit compliance. The actual financial transaction is finalized in person at the central Service Desk during QC handovers (cash or mobile transfers) to ensure maximum safety.
What is the image upload size limit? +
To maintain high performance and prevent abuse, we allow JPEG, PNG, GIF, and WEBP image files. Each file upload is limited to a maximum of 5MB.
Is there a review submission policy? +
Yes! To avoid artificial score inflating, customers can only write one review per product. Reviews are tied strictly to student IDs and verified purchases.
